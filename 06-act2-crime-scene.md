# Act 2, Crime Scene Handoff (Live Play Opener)

**When:** After **10-the gathering.md** (cast already briefed, Homes named primary suspect)  
**Runtime:** 30–45 minutes live interaction  
**Location:** OR + adjacent VIP corridor (your play space)  
**Goal:** Physical evidence search; wing split; first clue cards distributed

---

## NARRATOR SETUP (read aloud)

> The gathering is over. The charge is on the table: **Dr. Homes, primary suspect.**  
> Ray and Marz split the wing. OR sealed but searchable under their eyes, corridors assigned, no one walks alone.  
> The storm hasn't stopped. The police haven't come. The room has work to do.

*Players move to play space. OR still in red backup wash. Blood still drying.*

---

## SCENE 2, OR FORENSICS (scripted entrance)

*Ray, Marz, and Curie lead the group, or an assigned search party, back toward the theater. **HOMES** may be held at the lounge or brought to point without touching evidence.*

**RAY** tapes the threshold. Has already bagged the scalpel.

> **RAY:** Nobody touches the body. You look. You write it down. You tell me anything that says Homes didn't do it.

**CURIE** scans the floor, the trash, the ceiling mounts, too thorough for a liaison.

> **CURIE:** Start with what the lights didn't erase. Blood doesn't lie about order. Chemistry doesn't lie about timing.

*Curie is helpful, witty, warm, the room likes him and will chase him. **Homes** is the only one visibly irritated. **Pepper** may **ahem** if Ray and Curie crowd the same clue. Ray steers every helpful path that isn't Curie's keycard or his own lack of OR access.*

---

## DUE DILIGENCE, MUNDANE SECURITY BEATS

*Host: run these as short interactions players can overhear or participate in. Establishes realism.*

### 1. Perimeter seal
Ray tapes off corridor junction. Melody (if at desk down the hall) calls: *"Is the wing closed? I have a donor call on hold."*

Ray, without looking up: *"Wing's closed. Donor can wait."*

### 2. Chain of custody
Ray bags the scalpel **without** letting Homes touch it again.

> **RAY:** (to room) Full names. Where you stood when the red lights came on. Pepper, write the times. I write the names.

### 3. Radio, no patrol (storm)
Ray keys his radio:

> **RAY:** Guard shack, status on LC-4471.

*Static. Distorted reply:*

> **RADIO:** Barricade still holding at lower causeway… watch says… shelter in place… no ETA…

Ray clicks off, jaw tight.

> **RAY:** (to room) That's the city. We're the scene until they're not.

### 4. CCTV request (dead end)
Ray calls Vance on intercom:

> **RAY:** Vance. IT. Pre op to failover, *now*.

*CD Vance* (breathless):

> **VANCE:** VIP corridor three and OR internal are… there's gaps. Like something scrubbed the loop. Storm didn't do that. I'm pulling what I can.

> **RAY:** Gaps don't happen by weather. Note it.

*Plants hacking/CCTV red herring without naming a killer.*

### 5. Victim property
Ray gestures to De Worsti's folded clothes in the anteroom.

> **RAY:** Personal effects stay with watch until next of kin protocol. Search is limited to outer garments. Hospital counsel is already screaming.

*This is when players may discover **E1-C P.I. dossier** in coat jacket.*

### 6. Jetski hold
Ray to Jetski (gurney or sitting against wall):

> **RAY:** You're not arrested. You're also not leaving. Clinical witness. Pepper monitors. You say one word about dosing without me in the room, I'll put you in municipal cuffs anyway.

Jetski nods, pale.

## CURIE'S INVESTIGATION POSTURE (obvious red herring)

Curie doesn't pace. He *orients*, checks ceiling mounts, sight lines, scrub sink.

> **CURIE:** (quiet, to Ray) That wound won't read as panic. It's violent, almost crazed. But it's a scalpel. Who else in this building had attending privileges tonight?

> **RAY:** Everyone with a grudge and a badge. Starting with him.

*Gestures at Homes.*

> **CURIE:** Then we don't start with feeling. We start with floor patterns and who had vault access.

*Curie is helpful, and the room will mistake his motive for guilt.*

**Optional Curie line** (if players rush Sachs):

> **CURIE:** Street enforcers make noise. This room was silent. Don't waste your first hour on the loudest suspect.

## TRANSITION TO EPISODE 2

When blood pattern + vial + at least two testimonies are logged (or 30+ minutes elapsed):

**RAY** (closing the scene):

> OR's closed. De Worsti goes to the morgue. Everyone's statements are on paper.  
> Cops aren't here. I am. We get the vault records, who flipped the power, and whatever Vance swears he can't find, before those sirens show up.

**CURIE** (to players):

> Ten of you were inside when the lights died. One of you is lying well.  
> Officer Ray and I are taking statements in the recovery lounge.  
> Don't mistake cooperation for innocence.

*Narrator:*

> The storm kept hitting the glass. Municipal channels stayed empty. Somewhere two floors up, an administrative terminal held the ghost of a manual override. And somewhere in this wing, someone at the table already knew the wound looked too violent and crazed for a man still holding the blade when the red lights returned.
