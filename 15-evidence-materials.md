**Description:** OR floor diagram plus annotated note on blood found on Dr. Homes's surgical wear. Two distinct floor behaviors near the operating table, plus cast-off on the primary surgeon's gown. Suggests bleeding started before the blackout and was interrupted when the lights cut.

**Images:**

>
