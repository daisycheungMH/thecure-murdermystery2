# Player Dossiers, The Cure

Print **one dossier per player**. Dossiers are **private**, do not show other players.  
Format: **labeled sections** (Look, Vibe, Where, Goal, etc.) with content on the next line. **Likes**, **Dislikes**, **History**, and **Plan** use bullet lists (blank line after the label, then `-` items). **Rules** and **Night Truth** keep bullets where needed. No inline bold on names or keywords in body text.

**Punctuation:** No em dashes; use commas, colons, or ellipses for cuts in speech.

**Prose:** Prefer flowing sentences over staccato fragments. Combine related ideas with commas, *so*, *because*, or *and* instead of stacking three-line punches.

Also print **[player-performance-script.md](../player-performance-script.md)** (one copy for the table, or one per player). Players only read the act they are in.
