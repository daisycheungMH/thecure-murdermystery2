## THE TRUTH

**De Worsti** was a ruthless loan shark and crime lord. The Indigo syndicate knew him as **Purple Reign**. Bankrupt on paper, violent in practice, hunting his runaway ex wife to strip her of the fortune she stole when she fled. Tonight he booked **real** VIP arterial surgery at St. Saphicca's as a power play on Lacey's hospital. Genuine case. No illness stunt.

**Genevieve** was that wife. Ajax traded her to Alex as debt collateral; she grew up under Ajax's cruelty, then Alex's control. After Ray hunted her using police resources, Alex ordered **Uvula Sachs** to burn Ray's home. **Two dead:** Ray's mother and sister **Junie**. Genevieve vanished, underwent **total facial reconstruction**, and rebuilt herself as **Lacey Lovely**, hospital administrator of St. Saphicca's.

**Lacey Lovely** is Genevieve rebuilt. **Officer Ray** is her mother. In public they act like strangers who work in the same building. When Alex booked real surgery at her hospital, Lacey knew Ray had a plan. She coordinated logistics from admin: codes, wing lock, crisis posture two floors up. She never entered the OR. **Ray is the killer.** Lacey is a knowing accomplice on logistics if the room proves complicity.

**Dr. Curie** is Lacey's **university friend from the mid 1980s**, after Genevieve had already escaped and rebuilt herself. She learned what Alex did later, wanted De Worsti dead, and looks guilty in every way that matters, but **she did not make the cut**. **Curie is the obvious red herring**: university bond, surgical skill, charm, and motive make the room chase her while **Ray runs the investigation from the head of the table**. Playbook: [13-curie-charm-playbook.md](13-curie-charm-playbook.md)

**Dr. Homes** is the **first suspect** who must defend himself. Ray framed him at the scene with timing, blood, and a scalpel in the wrong gloves.

**Alfie De Worsti** (De Worsti's brother) is the player who **must reveal Genevieve's identity and the abuse** in Episode 4. Full backstory: [11-genevieve-backstory.md](11-genevieve-backstory.md)

**CD Vance** works for **Sachs under threat**, tracing De Worsti's wife through hospital/clinic systems for the stolen fortune. **De Worsti is revealed across four episodes as a bigger monster than his murderers:** [12-de-worsti-revelation-arc.md](12-de-worsti-revelation-arc.md)

## WHY RAY ACTED NOW

**Lacey** spent years believing De Worsti would never find St. Saphicca's. Then the warnings stacked up: **Uvula Sachs** on the VIP perimeter, collection faces in the parking structure, contractor queries spiking on reconstruction databases, a VIP intake that matched De Worsti's habits (sparse records, money buying priority). She was not paranoid. He was closing in.

**Ray** learned De Worsti had booked a **real** VIP arterial case at St. Saphicca's, compromised carotid wall, borderline rupture, genuinely life threatening if untreated. This was not a fake patient stunt. De Worsti needed the knife. He also chose **her** hospital on purpose: ego, leverage, and the bet that Genevieve was still too afraid to fight back.

**Ray had been waiting since the fire.** When De Worsti walked into Lacey's wing three years after Ray found her again, Lacey knew what Ray would need from admin. Ray accelerated a plan she had been building in secret: vault access, tampered vial, blackout, corridor entry, frame Homes. Murder was not a 48-hour panic by Curie. It was a mother's revenge, dressed as medical chaos, with Lacey's logistics upstairs and Ray's hand in the OR.

### Why the OR instead of elsewhere

Ray never planned to ambush Alex in a corridor, the parking structure, or the consultation suite.

| Constraint | Why it ruled out "elsewhere" |
|------------|------------------------------|
| **Sachs on perimeter** | VIP muscle on the wing; a hallway kill points at the security chief who escorted him in |
| **Cameras still live** | Storm had not yet given Ray cover; only the engineered blackout owned the blind spot |
| **Real arterial case** | If Ray waits, **Homes might save him**; a recovered Alex leaves with leverage and keeps hunting Lacey |
| **Ray's badge** | Security credentials do not open VIP OR Two; the stolen **Curie's keycard** only pays off at the OR reader |
| **The frame** | Paralyzed witnesses + bloody gloves and gown on Homes + theatre scalpel in hand + violent wound on tile only converge on the **table** as first read |
| **The alibi** | Dead VIP in a closet makes Ray explain why she was alone with the body; OR catastrophe lets her **run containment** |

Alex booked Lacey's hospital to own her room. Ray used that stage: sedated patient, chemically frozen staff, lights out on Ray's schedule, death that reads as OR rage. A security-chief murder would have been obvious anywhere else. Kill him elsewhere and Ray has a body, no frame, and a man who might still walk out.

Full context: [11-genevieve-backstory.md](11-genevieve-backstory.md)

## THE OPERATIONALLY TIGHT MURDER PLAN

| Step | Operation | Who | How |
|------|-----------|-----|-----|
| 1 | Drug Tampering | Officer Ray | Swapped anesthesia vial in secure pharmacy vault using security override + administrator emergency codes she memorized after finding Lacey |
| 2 | Sedative Delivery | Dr. Jetski (unknowing) | Injected tampered vial → backflow paralysis of Jetski + Nurse Pepper |
| 3 | Blackout Trigger | **Lacey Lovely** | Manual grid override from **admin office**; approximately one minute total darkness; primary log scrubbed post incident |
| 4 | OR Entry | Officer Ray | **Curie's stolen OR keycard** (Ray has no OR access on her own badge) + night vision gear from security stock + **red storm poncho** over two clothing layers |
| 5 | Execution | Officer Ray | Violent carotid cut with **disposable facial razor**; passionate and crazed in appearance; body thrown to tile |
| 6 | Frame Up | Officer Ray | Theatre **scalpel** left in disoriented Dr. Homes's grip; blood spatter on his gown from proximity and staging |
| 7 | Cover Up | Officer Ray | Strips red poncho into sharps refuse with bent razor; tore physical keycard log pre surgery; returned Curie's badge before she noticed; **mainframe shows Curie's card in OR** during blackout; pointed investigators at Curie (red herring) and Lacey (pharmacy/blackout admin tier) |

### Spatial configuration at blackout
**Inside OR:** Dr. Homes, Nurse Pepper, Dr. Jetski, De Worsti  
**Outside/remote:** **Lacey Lovely** (admin office, triggers override, scrubs primary logs post incident)  
**Inside OR (blackout):** **Officer Ray** (corridor entry, night vision, red poncho, facial razor kill, frame Homes with theatre scalpel)  
**Scattered:** All other players across VIP wing

### Kill mechanics (host only)
Ray wore a **red storm contingency poncho** over two clothing layers. She cut De Worsti's carotid with a **disposable facial razor** after dragging him to tile, then stripped the poncho and dropped it in **sharps and contaminated refuse** with the bent razor. Homes still reads guilty on night one because lights return with blood on his gloves and gown and a **theatre scalpel** in his hand. The actual kill tool surfaces in **E2-G** during Episode 2 deep search. Lacey never needed a red poncho or improvised steel; she was upstairs on the override. Melody's **E2-H** backup survives Lacey's deletion of the primary desk trail.

## FINAL BALLOT

**Targets:** Single suspect (**Officer Ray**) OR Pair (Ray + Lacey Lovely, only if room believes she knew)

**Moral verdict:**
- **MURDER**, criminal act; maximum punishment  
- **JUSTICE**, justified execution; cover it up  
- **SYSTEM FAILURE**, burn logs; expose hospital  

### Ending A, Truth exposed (Ray alone, or Ray + Lacey if room names both)
Room accuses correctly before sirens. Marz or Curie presents evidence to arriving watch. Full scripts: [05-episode-scripts.md](05-episode-scripts.md) → **AFTER THE BALLOT**.

Room accuses wrong person. Watch arrests the innocent in the chaos. Ray watches from the wing as patrol takes the fall guy.

Server room catastrophe. Vance + Homes killed. Watch arrives into rubble; Ray slips through recovery chaos.

**MURDER** / **JUSTICE** / **SYSTEM FAILURE**, read after accusation branch. Same file.
