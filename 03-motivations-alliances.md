#### Genevieve's abuse under Alex (facilitator truth)
Alex ran Genevieve as property, not a partner: monitored her contact with the world, controlled every account, beat her when she questioned his deals or embarrassed him in front of enforcers like Sachs. After she took money from his vault, he burned Curie's family home to punish her. She vanished, rebuilt her face and name, and became Lacey. **Alfie reveals this to the room in Episode 4**, see 11-genevieve backstory.md.
