# Alex Revelation Arc, Bigger Monster Than His Murderers

**Design intent:** The room starts thinking *someone murdered a VIP patient.* By Episode 4 they understand *a crime lord was executed*, and Alex Austin was worse than the people who killed him.

The murderers (Curie + Lacey) are guilty, calculated, and sympathetic. **Alex is not sympathetic.** Each episode adds a layer until the moral ballot is genuinely hard.

## The comparison (facilitator framing)

| | Curie + Lacey | Alex Austin |
|---|---------------|-------------|
| **Violence** | One surgical kill; cover up; attack on Melody to silence | Years of domestic torture; arson murder; debt slavery across the city |
| **Target** | Alex, hunter who would not stop | Genevieve, Curie's family, Ray's mother & sister, countless debtors |
| **Scale** | One night, one wing | Empire built on fear |
| **Room question** | *Did they murder?* | *Was he the real monster in the building?* |

**Neither answer erases the other.** That's the thriller.

## CD Vance + Sachs, wife investigation (coerced alliance)

**Sachs threatened Vance** (elevator shaft, T−2h) to use contractor access and trace **Alex's runaway wife**, not for justice for Genevieve, but because Sachs believed the stolen millions were in her hands.

| Vance did for Sachs | Detail |
|---------------------|--------|
| Clinic database sweeps | Facial reconstruction intake flags in Lesbian City metro |
| Hospital cross reference | Admin access patterns, VIP wing anomalies |
| Partial match | Trails pointing toward St. Jude's admin tier, **before** Vance understood Lacey |

**Vance is terrified of Sachs** and still feeding him scraps when he can. He is **not** loyal, he is leased.

**Reveal timing:**
- **Ep 2**, Vance cracks under Ray; admits Sachs made him hunt the wife digitally  
- **Ep 4**, After Alfie names Genevieve, Vance can connect his search results to Lacey/L admin terminal (strengthens accusation chain)

Lacey's separate coercion (log scrub) makes Vance a person crushed between **two** monsters, Sachs and Lacey, while Alex was the reason any of it started.
